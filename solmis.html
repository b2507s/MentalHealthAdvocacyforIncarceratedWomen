OUR MISSION & SOLUTIONS

Our Mission

We hope to educate, spread awareness of, and encourage activism to
improve mental health conditions and support for women in jail. Through
collaboration with other organizations, local leaders, and health centers,
we envision and hope for a future where all incarcerated and formerly
incarcerated women can have access to sufficient healthcare.
