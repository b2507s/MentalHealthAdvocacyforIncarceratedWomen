All clubs must hold at least 1 outreach and advocacy activity each month. Examples include:

  • Holding fundraising opportunities for MHAIW.
  • Organizing educational events at local community centers.
  • Promoting other organizations that are dedicated to supporting incarcerated women.
  • Collaborating with a local jail to communicate with real individuals.
  • Implementing a school-wide, county-wide, or state-wide initiative.
  • Leading a donation or other resource-related event.
